Cormetech, a leading player in the design, manufacture, and full lifecycle management of technologies that clean the air of pollutants and carbon dioxide (CO2) linked to climate change, is delighted to shine a spotlight on one of its exceptional employees. Today, we proudly present Scott Pritchard, a dedicated Senior Engineer whose innovative spirit and unwavering commitment to environmental sustainability have significantly contributed to Cormetech's success.

Driving Innovation and Clean Air Technologies

Scott goes beyond technical expertise by actively promoting sustainability and eco-conscious practices within Cormetech. Collaborating with cross-functional teams, he strives to reduce the company's environmental footprint, ensuring that operations align with the mission of protecting the planet.

Scott's efforts extend to internal initiatives aimed at minimizing waste, conserving energy, and promoting recycling practices throughout the company. By fostering a culture of environmental responsibility, Scott inspires his colleagues to adopt sustainable practices both inside and outside the workplace.
